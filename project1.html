Description

This project involves the in-depth analysis and optimization of hydrodynamic drag in deep-sea submarines. Utilizing ANSYS and Fusion 360, we aimed to enhance the efficiency and performance of underwater vehicles by minimizing drag through advanced computational simulations and design optimizations.

Tools Used

  • ANSYS
  • Fusion 360

Results

The project resulted in significant improvements in drag reduction, leading to enhanced underwater maneuverability and increased efficiency of deep-sea submarines. Detailed simulations and optimizations contributed to a better understanding of hydrodynamic forces, aiding in future submarine design enhancements.
